Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information that you provide to us through our app "Simple Finance - Take Control" that links to this privacy policy.

1. Data Collection

Local Transaction Data:

We collect transaction data that you input into the Service. This includes:

  • Transaction Amount: The amount of money involved in the transaction.
  • Transaction Type: Whether this is income or expenditure.
  • Transaction Description: A short text description of the transaction such as "Groceries," "Salary," "Coffee."

No PII:

We do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers, browsing history, or purchase history.

2. Data Usage

Local Transaction Management:

The transaction data collected does not leave the Service and is used locally for the following purposes:

  • Local Storage: Transactions are stored locally on your device.
  • Tracking Transactions: You can view your transaction history and add new ones within the Service.
  • No Data Sharing: No data regarding any transactions made would be shared with third parties, advertisers, data brokers, or other companies.

3. Data Storage

Local Storage: Your transaction data is locally stored on your device. We do not store any data related to your transactions on our servers.

4. Data Security

Local Security: Your transaction data's security depends solely on the security measures that you have put in place on your device. You are advised to take proper steps to safeguard your device against unauthorized access.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
